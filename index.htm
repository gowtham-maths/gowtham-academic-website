About Me

I am a doctoral researcher in the Division of Mathematics, School of Advanced Sciences at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Chennai. My Ph.D. research focuses on Systems and Control Theory. I have worked on mathematical modeling, control design, and event-triggered systems. I am also actively engaged in projects funded by TNSCST, exploring school students' perception towards mathematics.

Research Interests

  • Systems and Control Theory
  • Takagi–Sugeno (T-S) Fuzzy Systems
  • Load Frequency Control in Power Systems
  • Event-triggered Control & Synchronization
  • Mathematical Modeling & Stability Analysis
